Payment Methods

At Graven Automation, we strive to make your purchasing experience as smooth and secure as possible. That’s why we support a wide range of trusted payment options to suit your convenience:


All Major Credit and Debit Cards: We accept Visa, MasterCard, RuPay, American Express, and more. Whether you're using a domestic or international card, your transaction is processed securely with end-to-end encryption.

UPI Payments: Enjoy fast and hassle-free payments through popular UPI platforms such as Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, and BHIM. UPI offers instant confirmation and is one of the most convenient ways to pay.

Net Banking: Choose from a wide list of supported Indian banks to complete your payment directly from your bank account. Our platform supports secure and real-time net banking transactions.

All transactions on our website are processed through secure payment gateways to ensure your data and money are protected. No sensitive information is ever stored on our servers.


We are committed to providing a safe, transparent, and flexible payment experience for all our customers.
